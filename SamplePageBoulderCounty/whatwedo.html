About Us

ASBC is an opportunity generator for people on the autism spectrum and their families, focused on lifelong support, recreation, and learning gaps in Boulder County. Our programs strive to meet the needs of all ages, ethnicities, cultures, genders, orientations, and ability levels. We encourage participation by creating a low-cost, friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for as many community events as possible. Our events range from "bridging" events for participation by people with autism only to fully inclusive events in the community. "Bridging" events are designed as opportunities for people on the spectrum to try activities that might lead to a full inclusion event in the future. All of our programs and events are intended to provide a bridge to full inclusion in the future for people who may currently feel most comfortable in events exclusive to people on the autism spectrum or to families who may need to try something new in a low anxiety, easy to access environment prior to participating in a similar activity in the general community. Our events are designed to be as sensory-friendly as possible. They are always judgment-free, accepting of the challenges and appreciative of the gifts autism has to offer.
